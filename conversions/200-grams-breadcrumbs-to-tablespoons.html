How we convert 200 grams of breadcrumbs to tablespoons

Breadcrumbs has a density of about 6.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 6.8 = 29.41. So 200 grams of breadcrumbs equals 29.41 tablespoons.

Dry breadcrumbs weigh about 108g per cup. Panko breadcrumbs are lighter; fresh breadcrumbs are heavier.
