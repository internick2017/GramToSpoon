How we convert 5 grams of salt to teaspoons

Salt has a density of about 6 grams per teaspoon. So to convert 5 grams we divide: 5 ÷ 6 = 0.83. So 5 grams of salt equals 0.83 teaspoons.

Table salt (fine) weighs about 6g per teaspoon. Kosher salt is lighter per teaspoon — roughly 3-5g depending on brand.
