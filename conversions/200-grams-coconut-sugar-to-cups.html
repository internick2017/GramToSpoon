How we convert 200 grams of coconut sugar to cups

Coconut Sugar has a density of about 180 grams per cup. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 180 = 1.11. So 200 grams of coconut sugar equals 1.11 cups.

Coconut sugar has a coarser texture than white sugar. It weighs about 180g per cup — a bit lighter than granulated.
