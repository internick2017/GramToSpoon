How we convert 100 grams of rolled oats to cups

Rolled Oats has a density of about 90 grams per cup. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 90 = 1.11. So 100 grams of rolled oats equals 1.11 cups.

Old-fashioned rolled oats are light and airy at about 90g per cup. Quick oats are slightly denser.
