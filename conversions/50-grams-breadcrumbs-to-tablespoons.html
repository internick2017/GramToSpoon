How we convert 50 grams of breadcrumbs to tablespoons

Breadcrumbs has a density of about 6.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 6.8 = 7.35. So 50 grams of breadcrumbs equals 7.35 tablespoons.

Dry breadcrumbs weigh about 108g per cup. Panko breadcrumbs are lighter; fresh breadcrumbs are heavier.
