How we convert 50 grams of whole wheat flour to tablespoons

Whole Wheat Flour has a density of about 8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 8 = 6.25. So 50 grams of whole wheat flour equals 6.25 tablespoons.

Whole wheat flour is denser than white flours because it contains the bran and germ. Spoon and level for accuracy.
