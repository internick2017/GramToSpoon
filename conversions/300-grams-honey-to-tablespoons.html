How we convert 300 grams of honey to tablespoons

Honey has a density of about 21.3 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 300 grams we divide: 300 ÷ 21.3 = 14.08. So 300 grams of honey equals 14.08 tablespoons.

Honey is dense and sticky. One tablespoon weighs about 21g. Spray your measuring spoon with oil first so the honey slides off easily.
