How we convert 25 grams of baking powder to tablespoons

Baking Powder has a density of about 13.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 25 grams we divide: 25 ÷ 13.8 = 1.81. So 25 grams of baking powder equals 1.81 tablespoons.

Baking powder weighs about 4.6g per teaspoon. Check the expiration date — old baking powder loses its leavening power.
