How we convert 100 grams of coconut flour to tablespoons

Coconut Flour has a density of about 8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 8 = 12.50. So 100 grams of coconut flour equals 12.50 tablespoons.

Coconut flour is very absorbent — you typically need much less than wheat flour. It weighs about 128g per cup.
