How we convert 300 grams of brown sugar to tablespoons

Brown Sugar has a density of about 13.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 300 grams we divide: 300 ÷ 13.8 = 21.74. So 300 grams of brown sugar equals 21.74 tablespoons.

Brown sugar is typically measured packed. One packed cup weighs about 220g. Light and dark brown sugar weigh the same.
