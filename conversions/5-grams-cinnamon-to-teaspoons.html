How we convert 5 grams of cinnamon to teaspoons

Cinnamon has a density of about 2.6 grams per teaspoon. So to convert 5 grams we divide: 5 ÷ 2.6 = 1.92. So 5 grams of cinnamon equals 1.92 teaspoons.

Ground cinnamon weighs about 2.6g per teaspoon. Use level teaspoons for consistent flavor.
