So 100g of rice is about 8.70 tablespoons.

How we convert 100 grams of rice to tablespoons

Rice has a density of about 11.5 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 11.5 = 8.70. So 100 grams of rice equals 8.70 tablespoons.

Uncooked long-grain white rice; short-grain and brown rice may differ slightly. Always measure before cooking.
