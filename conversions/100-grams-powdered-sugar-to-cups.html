How we convert 100 grams of powdered sugar to cups

Powdered Sugar has a density of about 120 grams per cup. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 120 = 0.83. So 100 grams of powdered sugar equals 0.83 cups.

Powdered (confectioners') sugar is very light and fluffy. Sift before measuring for consistent results — one unsifted cup is about 120g.
