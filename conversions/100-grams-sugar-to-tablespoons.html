How we convert 100 grams of granulated sugar to tablespoons

Granulated Sugar has a density of about 12.5 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 12.5 = 8. So 100 grams of granulated sugar equals 8 tablespoons.

Granulated white sugar has a density of about 12.5 grams per tablespoon. Use level tablespoons (not heaped) for best accuracy.
