How we convert 100 grams of almonds to cups

Almonds has a density of about 143 grams per cup. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 143 = 0.70. So 100 grams of almonds equals 0.70 cups.

Whole almonds weigh about 143g per cup. Sliced or slivered almonds are lighter per cup due to air gaps.
