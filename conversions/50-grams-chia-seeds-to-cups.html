How we convert 50 grams of chia seeds to cups

Chia Seeds has a density of about 163 grams per cup. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 163 = 0.31. So 50 grams of chia seeds equals 0.31 cups.

Chia seeds weigh about 163g per cup. They absorb many times their weight in liquid, so measure before soaking.
