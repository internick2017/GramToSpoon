How we convert 200 grams of vegetable oil to tablespoons

Vegetable Oil has a density of about 13.6 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 13.6 = 14.71. So 200 grams of vegetable oil equals 14.71 tablespoons.

Vegetable oil (canola, sunflower, etc.) weighs about 218g per cup. Most neutral cooking oils have similar density.
