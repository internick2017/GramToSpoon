How we convert 50 grams of baking powder to tablespoons

Baking Powder has a density of about 13.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 13.8 = 3.62. So 50 grams of baking powder equals 3.62 tablespoons.

Baking powder weighs about 4.6g per teaspoon. Check the expiration date — old baking powder loses its leavening power.
