How we convert 500 grams of cake flour to tablespoons

Cake Flour has a density of about 7.1 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 500 grams we divide: 500 ÷ 7.1 = 70.42. So 500 grams of cake flour equals 70.42 tablespoons.

Cake flour is lighter and finer than all-purpose. It weighs about 114g per cup when spooned and leveled.
