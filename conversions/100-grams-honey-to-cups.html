How we convert 100 grams of honey to cups

Honey has a density of about 340 grams per cup. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 340 = 0.29. So 100 grams of honey equals 0.29 cups.

Honey is dense and sticky. One tablespoon weighs about 21g. Spray your measuring spoon with oil first so the honey slides off easily.
