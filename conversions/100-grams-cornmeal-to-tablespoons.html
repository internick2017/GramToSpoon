How we convert 100 grams of cornmeal to tablespoons

Cornmeal has a density of about 9.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 9.8 = 10.20. So 100 grams of cornmeal equals 10.20 tablespoons.

Medium-grind yellow cornmeal weighs about 157g per cup. Fine-grind cornmeal may be slightly denser.
