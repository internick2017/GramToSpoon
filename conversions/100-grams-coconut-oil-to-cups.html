How we convert 100 grams of coconut oil to cups

Coconut Oil has a density of about 218 grams per cup. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 218 = 0.46. So 100 grams of coconut oil equals 0.46 cups.

Coconut oil is solid at room temperature. Melt it first for accurate measuring by volume, or weigh for best results.
