How we convert 200 grams of cornmeal to tablespoons

Cornmeal has a density of about 9.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 9.8 = 20.41. So 200 grams of cornmeal equals 20.41 tablespoons.

Medium-grind yellow cornmeal weighs about 157g per cup. Fine-grind cornmeal may be slightly denser.
