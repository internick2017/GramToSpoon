How we convert 200 grams of condensed milk to tablespoons

Condensed Milk has a density of about 19.1 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 19.1 = 10.47. So 200 grams of condensed milk equals 10.47 tablespoons.

Sweetened condensed milk is thick and heavy at about 306g per cup. One standard can (14 oz) is about 397g.
