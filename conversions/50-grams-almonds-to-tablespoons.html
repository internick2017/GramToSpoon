How we convert 50 grams of almonds to tablespoons

Almonds has a density of about 8.9 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 8.9 = 5.62. So 50 grams of almonds equals 5.62 tablespoons.

Whole almonds weigh about 143g per cup. Sliced or slivered almonds are lighter per cup due to air gaps.
