How we convert 100 grams of sesame seeds to tablespoons

Sesame Seeds has a density of about 9 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 9 = 11.11. So 100 grams of sesame seeds equals 11.11 tablespoons.

Sesame seeds (white or black, unhulled) weigh about 144g per cup.
