How we convert 100 grams of couscous to tablespoons

Couscous has a density of about 10.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 10.8 = 9.26. So 100 grams of couscous equals 9.26 tablespoons.

Uncooked regular couscous weighs about 173g per cup. Israeli (pearl) couscous is heavier.
