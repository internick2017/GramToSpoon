How we convert 100 grams of walnuts to tablespoons

Walnuts has a density of about 7.5 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 7.5 = 13.33. So 100 grams of walnuts equals 13.33 tablespoons.

Chopped walnuts weigh about 120g per cup. Halves will weigh slightly less per cup due to irregular shapes.
