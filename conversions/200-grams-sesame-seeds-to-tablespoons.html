How we convert 200 grams of sesame seeds to tablespoons

Sesame Seeds has a density of about 9 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 9 = 22.22. So 200 grams of sesame seeds equals 22.22 tablespoons.
