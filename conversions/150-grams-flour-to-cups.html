How we convert 150 grams of all-purpose flour to cups

All-Purpose Flour has a density of about 120 grams per cup. So to convert 150 grams we divide: 150 ÷ 120 = 1.25. So 150 grams of all-purpose flour equals 1.25 cups.

All-purpose flour can vary by brand; we use the standard 120g per cup (spooned and leveled). Don't scoop directly from the bag — spoon into your cup and level off for consistent results.
