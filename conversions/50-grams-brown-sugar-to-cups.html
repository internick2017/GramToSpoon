How we convert 50 grams of brown sugar to cups

Brown Sugar has a density of about 220 grams per cup. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 220 = 0.23. So 50 grams of brown sugar equals 0.23 cups.

Brown sugar is typically measured packed. One packed cup weighs about 220g. Light and dark brown sugar weigh the same.
