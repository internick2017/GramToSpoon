How we convert 200 grams of all-purpose flour to tablespoons

All-Purpose Flour has a density of about 7.5 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 7.5 = 26.67. So 200 grams of all-purpose flour equals 26.67 tablespoons.

All-purpose flour can vary by brand; we use the standard 120g per cup (spooned and leveled). Don't scoop directly from the bag — spoon into your cup and level off for consistent results.
