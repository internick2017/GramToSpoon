How we convert 50 grams of cinnamon to tablespoons

Cinnamon has a density of about 7.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 7.8 = 6.41. So 50 grams of cinnamon equals 6.41 tablespoons.

Ground cinnamon weighs about 2.6g per teaspoon. Use level teaspoons for consistent flavor.
