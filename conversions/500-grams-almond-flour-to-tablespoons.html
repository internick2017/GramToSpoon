How we convert 500 grams of almond flour to tablespoons

Almond Flour has a density of about 6 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 500 grams we divide: 500 ÷ 6 = 83.33. So 500 grams of almond flour equals 83.33 tablespoons.

Almond flour (blanched, finely ground) is lighter than wheat flours. Pack gently — don't compress — when measuring by cup.
