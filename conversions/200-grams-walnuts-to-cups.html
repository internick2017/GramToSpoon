How we convert 200 grams of walnuts to cups

Walnuts has a density of about 120 grams per cup. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 120 = 1.67. So 200 grams of walnuts equals 1.67 cups.

Chopped walnuts weigh about 120g per cup. Halves will weigh slightly less per cup due to irregular shapes.
