How we convert 75 grams of butter to tablespoons

Butter has a density of about 14.2 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 75 grams we divide: 75 ÷ 14.2 = 5.28. So 75 grams of butter equals 5.28 tablespoons.

One standard stick of butter is 113g (1/2 cup or 8 tablespoons). Use the markings on the wrapper for easy measuring.
