How we convert 5 grams of cinnamon to tablespoons

Cinnamon has a density of about 7.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 5 grams we divide: 5 ÷ 7.8 = 0.64. So 5 grams of cinnamon equals 0.64 tablespoons.

Ground cinnamon weighs about 2.6g per teaspoon. Use level teaspoons for consistent flavor.
