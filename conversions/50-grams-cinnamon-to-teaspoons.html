How we convert 50 grams of cinnamon to teaspoons

Cinnamon has a density of about 2.6 grams per teaspoon. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 2.6 = 19.23. So 50 grams of cinnamon equals 19.23 teaspoons.

Ground cinnamon weighs about 2.6g per teaspoon. Use level teaspoons for consistent flavor.
