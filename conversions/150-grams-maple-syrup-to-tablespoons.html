How we convert 150 grams of maple syrup to tablespoons

Maple Syrup has a density of about 19.7 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 150 grams we divide: 150 ÷ 19.7 = 7.61. So 150 grams of maple syrup equals 7.61 tablespoons.

Pure maple syrup is slightly less dense than honey. Use a liquid measuring cup for larger amounts.
