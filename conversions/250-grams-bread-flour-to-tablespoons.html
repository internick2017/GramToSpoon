How we convert 250 grams of bread flour to tablespoons

Bread Flour has a density of about 7.9 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 250 grams we divide: 250 ÷ 7.9 = 31.65. So 250 grams of bread flour equals 31.65 tablespoons.

Bread flour is slightly denser than all-purpose flour due to its higher protein content. Spoon and level for best accuracy.
