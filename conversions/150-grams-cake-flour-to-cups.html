How we convert 150 grams of cake flour to cups

Cake Flour has a density of about 114 grams per cup. So to convert 150 grams we divide: 150 ÷ 114 = 1.32. So 150 grams of cake flour equals 1.32 cups.

Cake flour is lighter and finer than all-purpose. It weighs about 114g per cup when spooned and leveled.
