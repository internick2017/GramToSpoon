How we convert 500 grams of whole wheat flour to cups

Whole Wheat Flour has a density of about 128 grams per cup. So to convert 500 grams we divide: 500 ÷ 128 = 3.91. So 500 grams of whole wheat flour equals 3.91 cups.

Whole wheat flour is denser than white flours because it contains the bran and germ. Spoon and level for accuracy.
