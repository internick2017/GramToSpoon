How we convert 50 grams of self-rising flour to cups

Self-Rising Flour has a density of about 125 grams per cup. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 125 = 0.40. So 50 grams of self-rising flour equals 0.40 cups.

Self-rising flour includes baking powder and salt. It weighs about 125g per cup, similar to all-purpose flour.
