How we convert 200 grams of condensed milk to cups

Condensed Milk has a density of about 306 grams per cup. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 306 = 0.65. So 200 grams of condensed milk equals 0.65 cups.

Sweetened condensed milk is thick and heavy at about 306g per cup. One standard can (14 oz) is about 397g.
