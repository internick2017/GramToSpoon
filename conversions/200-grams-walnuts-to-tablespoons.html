How we convert 200 grams of walnuts to tablespoons

Walnuts has a density of about 7.5 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 7.5 = 26.67. So 200 grams of walnuts equals 26.67 tablespoons.

Chopped walnuts weigh about 120g per cup. Halves will weigh slightly less per cup due to irregular shapes.
