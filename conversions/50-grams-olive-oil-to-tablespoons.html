How we convert 50 grams of olive oil to tablespoons

Olive Oil has a density of about 13.5 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 13.5 = 3.70. So 50 grams of olive oil equals 3.70 tablespoons.

Olive oil is lighter than water — about 13.5g per tablespoon. Use a liquid measuring cup for larger amounts.
