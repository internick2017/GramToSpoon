How we convert 100 grams of cornstarch to cups

Cornstarch has a density of about 128 grams per cup. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 128 = 0.78. So 100 grams of cornstarch equals 0.78 cups.

Cornstarch (cornflour in the UK) is very fine and powdery. Spoon into your measuring cup and level off — don't pack.
