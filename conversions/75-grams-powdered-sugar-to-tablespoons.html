How we convert 75 grams of powdered sugar to tablespoons

Powdered Sugar has a density of about 7.5 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 75 grams we divide: 75 ÷ 7.5 = 10. So 75 grams of powdered sugar equals 10 tablespoons.

Powdered (confectioners') sugar is very light and fluffy. Sift before measuring for consistent results — one unsifted cup is about 120g.
