How we convert 250 grams of butter to cups

Butter has a density of about 227 grams per cup. So to convert 250 grams we divide: 250 ÷ 227 = 1.10. So 250 grams of butter equals 1.10 cups.

One standard stick of butter is 113g (1/2 cup or 8 tablespoons). Use the markings on the wrapper for easy measuring.
