How we convert 150 grams of granulated sugar to tablespoons

Granulated Sugar has a density of about 12.5 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 150 grams we divide: 150 ÷ 12.5 = 12. So 150 grams of granulated sugar equals 12 tablespoons.

Granulated white sugar has a density of about 12.5 grams per tablespoon. Use level tablespoons (not heaped) for best accuracy.
