How we convert 100 grams of almonds to tablespoons

Almonds has a density of about 8.9 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 8.9 = 11.24. So 100 grams of almonds equals 11.24 tablespoons.

Whole almonds weigh about 143g per cup. Sliced or slivered almonds are lighter per cup due to air gaps.
