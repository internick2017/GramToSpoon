How we convert 50 grams of yogurt to tablespoons

Yogurt has a density of about 15.3 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 15.3 = 3.27. So 50 grams of yogurt equals 3.27 tablespoons.

Plain yogurt (regular or Greek) weighs about 245g per cup, similar to milk. Greek yogurt may be slightly denser.
