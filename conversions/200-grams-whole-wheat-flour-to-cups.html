How we convert 200 grams of whole wheat flour to cups

Whole Wheat Flour has a density of about 128 grams per cup. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 128 = 1.56. So 200 grams of whole wheat flour equals 1.56 cups.

Whole wheat flour is denser than white flours because it contains the bran and germ. Spoon and level for accuracy.
