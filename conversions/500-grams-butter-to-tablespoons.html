How we convert 500 grams of butter to tablespoons

Butter has a density of about 14.2 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 500 grams we divide: 500 ÷ 14.2 = 35.21. So 500 grams of butter equals 35.21 tablespoons.

One standard stick of butter is 113g (1/2 cup or 8 tablespoons). Use the markings on the wrapper for easy measuring.
