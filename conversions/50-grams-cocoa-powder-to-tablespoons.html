How we convert 50 grams of cocoa powder to tablespoons

Cocoa Powder has a density of about 5.4 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 5.4 = 9.26. So 50 grams of cocoa powder equals 9.26 tablespoons.

Unsweetened cocoa powder is very light — only about 86g per cup. Sift before measuring to remove lumps.
