How we convert 100 grams of water to tablespoons

Water has a density of about 14.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 14.8 = 6.76. So 100 grams of water equals 6.76 tablespoons.

Water weighs about 237g per US cup (slightly less than 250ml). Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy.
