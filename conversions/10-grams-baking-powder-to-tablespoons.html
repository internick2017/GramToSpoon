How we convert 10 grams of baking powder to tablespoons

Baking Powder has a density of about 13.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 10 grams we divide: 10 ÷ 13.8 = 0.72. So 10 grams of baking powder equals 0.72 tablespoons.

Baking powder weighs about 4.6g per teaspoon. Check the expiration date — old baking powder loses its leavening power.
