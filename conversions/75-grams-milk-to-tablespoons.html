How we convert 75 grams of milk to tablespoons

Milk has a density of about 15.3 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 75 grams we divide: 75 ÷ 15.3 = 4.90. So 75 grams of milk equals 4.90 tablespoons.

Whole milk; skim and low-fat milk have very similar density. Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy.
