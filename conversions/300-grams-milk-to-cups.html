How we convert 300 grams of milk to cups

Milk has a density of about 245 grams per cup. So to convert 300 grams we divide: 300 ÷ 245 = 1.22. So 300 grams of milk equals 1.22 cups.

Whole milk; skim and low-fat milk have very similar density. Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy.
