How we convert 250 grams of cake flour to cups

Cake Flour has a density of about 114 grams per cup. So to convert 250 grams we divide: 250 ÷ 114 = 2.19. So 250 grams of cake flour equals 2.19 cups.

Cake flour is lighter and finer than all-purpose. It weighs about 114g per cup when spooned and leveled.
