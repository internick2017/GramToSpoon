How we convert 200 grams of cocoa powder to tablespoons

Cocoa Powder has a density of about 5.4 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 5.4 = 37.04. So 200 grams of cocoa powder equals 37.04 tablespoons.

Unsweetened cocoa powder is very light — only about 86g per cup. Sift before measuring to remove lumps.
