How we convert 50 grams of chia seeds to tablespoons

Chia Seeds has a density of about 10.2 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 50 grams we divide: 50 ÷ 10.2 = 4.90. So 50 grams of chia seeds equals 4.90 tablespoons.

Chia seeds weigh about 163g per cup. They absorb many times their weight in liquid, so measure before soaking.
