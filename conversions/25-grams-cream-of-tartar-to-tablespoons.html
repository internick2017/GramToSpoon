How we convert 25 grams of cream of tartar to tablespoons

Cream of Tartar has a density of about 9 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 25 grams we divide: 25 ÷ 9 = 2.78. So 25 grams of cream of tartar equals 2.78 tablespoons.

Cream of tartar (potassium bitartrate) weighs about 3g per teaspoon. It stabilizes egg whites and activates baking soda.
