How we convert 10 grams of cinnamon to teaspoons

Cinnamon has a density of about 2.6 grams per teaspoon. So to convert 10 grams we divide: 10 ÷ 2.6 = 3.85. So 10 grams of cinnamon equals 3.85 teaspoons.

Ground cinnamon weighs about 2.6g per teaspoon. Use level teaspoons for consistent flavor.
