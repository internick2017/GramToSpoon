How we convert 500 grams of butter to cups

Butter has a density of about 227 grams per cup. So to convert 500 grams we divide: 500 ÷ 227 = 2.20. So 500 grams of butter equals 2.20 cups.

One standard stick of butter is 113g (1/2 cup or 8 tablespoons). Use the markings on the wrapper for easy measuring.
