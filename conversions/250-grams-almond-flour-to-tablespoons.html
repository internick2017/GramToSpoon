How we convert 250 grams of almond flour to tablespoons

Almond Flour has a density of about 6 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 250 grams we divide: 250 ÷ 6 = 41.67. So 250 grams of almond flour equals 41.67 tablespoons.

Almond flour (blanched, finely ground) is lighter than wheat flours. Pack gently — don't compress — when measuring by cup.
