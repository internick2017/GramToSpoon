How we convert 10 grams of cinnamon to tablespoons

Cinnamon has a density of about 7.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 10 grams we divide: 10 ÷ 7.8 = 1.28. So 10 grams of cinnamon equals 1.28 tablespoons.

Ground cinnamon weighs about 2.6g per teaspoon. Use level teaspoons for consistent flavor.
