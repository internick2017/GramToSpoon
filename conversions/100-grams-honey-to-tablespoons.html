How we convert 100 grams of honey to tablespoons

Honey has a density of about 21.3 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 21.3 = 4.69. So 100 grams of honey equals 4.69 tablespoons.

Honey is dense and sticky. One tablespoon weighs about 21g. Spray your measuring spoon with oil first so the honey slides off easily.
