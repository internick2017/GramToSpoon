How we convert 500 grams of brown sugar to cups

Brown Sugar has a density of about 220 grams per cup. So to convert 500 grams we divide: 500 ÷ 220 = 2.27. So 500 grams of brown sugar equals 2.27 cups.

Brown sugar is typically measured packed. One packed cup weighs about 220g. Light and dark brown sugar weigh the same.
