How we convert 200 grams of sunflower seeds to tablespoons

Sunflower Seeds has a density of about 8.8 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 200 grams we divide: 200 ÷ 8.8 = 22.73. So 200 grams of sunflower seeds equals 22.73 tablespoons.
