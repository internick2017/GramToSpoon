How we convert 250 grams of milk to tablespoons

Milk has a density of about 15.3 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 250 grams we divide: 250 ÷ 15.3 = 16.34. So 250 grams of milk equals 16.34 tablespoons.

Whole milk; skim and low-fat milk have very similar density. Use a liquid measuring cup for accuracy.
