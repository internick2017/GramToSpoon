How we convert 100 grams of chocolate chips to cups

Chocolate Chips has a density of about 170 grams per cup. So to convert 100 grams we divide: 100 ÷ 170 = 0.59. So 100 grams of chocolate chips equals 0.59 cups.

Standard semi-sweet chocolate chips weigh about 170g per cup. Mini chips pack more densely per cup.
