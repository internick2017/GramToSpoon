How we convert 250 grams of chocolate chips to tablespoons

Chocolate Chips has a density of about 10.6 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 250 grams we divide: 250 ÷ 10.6 = 23.58. So 250 grams of chocolate chips equals 23.58 tablespoons.

Standard semi-sweet chocolate chips weigh about 170g per cup. Mini chips pack more densely per cup.
