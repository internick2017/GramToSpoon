How we convert 500 grams of honey to tablespoons

Honey has a density of about 21.3 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 500 grams we divide: 500 ÷ 21.3 = 23.47. So 500 grams of honey equals 23.47 tablespoons.

Honey is dense and sticky. One tablespoon weighs about 21g. Spray your measuring spoon with oil first so the honey slides off easily.
