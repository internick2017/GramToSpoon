So 25g of baking soda is about 1.39 tablespoons.

How we convert 25 grams of baking soda to tablespoons

Baking Soda has a density of about 18 grams per tablespoon. So to convert 25 grams we divide: 25 ÷ 18 = 1.39. So 25 grams of baking soda equals 1.39 tablespoons.

Baking soda (sodium bicarbonate) is denser than baking powder at about 6g per teaspoon. A little goes a long way.
